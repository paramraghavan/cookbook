Use Black Salt (Kala Namak) as a Finisher

Finish salads, cucumbers, tomatoes, or watermelon with black salt instead of regular salt. Its sulfur compounds provide a savory umami depth that enhances flavor perception.

Soak Onions in Ice Water Before Cutting

Soak onions in ice water for 10 minutes before chopping. The cold water dissolves sulfur compounds and slows the enzymatic reaction responsible for tearing.

Add Baking Soda to Dried Lentils and Beans

Add about ¼ teaspoon baking soda per cup of beans. Raising the pH weakens pectin in the cell walls, dramatically reducing cooking time.
