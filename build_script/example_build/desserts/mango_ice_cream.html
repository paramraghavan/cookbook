Mango Ice Cream

Creamy, delicious homemade mango ice cream - no ice cream maker needed!

Ingredients

  • 3 ripe mangoes (about 2 cups mango pulp)
  • 2 cups heavy whipping cream (chilled)
  • 1 cup sweetened condensed milk
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • Pinch of salt

Instructions

  1. Peel and chop the mangoes, then puree them in a blender until smooth
  2. In a large bowl, whip the heavy cream until stiff peaks form (about 5 minutes)
  3. In another bowl, mix the mango puree, condensed milk, vanilla, and salt
  4. Gently fold the mango mixture into the whipped cream
  5. Pour into a freezer-safe container
  6. Cover with plastic wrap and freeze for at least 6 hours or overnight
  7. Let sit at room temperature for 5 minutes before scooping

Cooking Time

  • Prep: 15 minutes
  • Freeze: 6 hours
  • Total: 6 hours 15 minutes

Servings

6-8 servings

Tips

  • Use ripe, sweet mangoes for the best flavor
  • Alphonso or Kesar mangoes work wonderfully
  • For extra creaminess, use full-fat cream
  • Add small mango chunks for texture
  • Can be stored in freezer for up to 1 month

Variations

  • Mango Cardamom: Add 1/4 teaspoon ground cardamom
  • Mango Swirl: Don't mix completely for a marbled effect
  • Mango Pistachio: Add chopped pistachios before freezing
