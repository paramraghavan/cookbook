# Indian Style Pasta

**Ingredients:**

- **Pasta:** 2 cups (spiral, fusilli, or any)
- **Water:** Enough to boil pasta (about 4-5 cups), and for the Instant Pot, 2 cups (enough to just cover pasta)
- **Salt:** 1 tsp for boiling pasta + 1/2 tsp for masala(optional) (adjust to taste)
- **Oil:** 1 tsp Olive oil  (any neutral oil)
- **Butter:** 1 tbsp

**Vegetables & Base:**
- **Onion:** 1 medium, chopped
- **Green Chilis:** 1-2, finely chopped (optional, adjust to preferred spice)
- **Ginger Garlic Paste:** 2 tsp (or 2 tsp finely chopped) - optional
- **Carrot:** 1 small, chopped
- **Capsicum (Bell Pepper):** 1 small, chopped
- **Boiled Corn:** 2-3 tbsp
- **Beans:** 4-5, chopped (optional)
- **Tomato:** 1 medium, chopped
- **Pasta sauce:** 1/2 to 1 cup to your taste
- **Cashew:** 5-10, powder it, optional

**Spices:**
- **Red Chili Powder:** 1 tsp (adjust for heat)
- **Coriander Powder:** 1/2 tsp
- **Garam Masala:** 1/4 tsp (optional)
- **Mustard seeds** 1 tsp
- **Urad Dal** 1/2 tsp 

**Sauce & Finish:**
- **Pasta Sauce (or Tomato Sauce):** 2-3 tbsp
- **Grated Cheese:** 1/4 -1/2 cup (or more for topping)
- **Fresh Coriander:** 2 tbsp, chopped
- **Mixed Herbs Seasoning:** 1/2 tsp
- **Red Chili Flakes:** 1/2 tsp (optional, for garnish)

Adjust veggie amounts and spice to taste and as per what you have! This will make a balanced, flavorful masala pasta.

**Steps:**

1. **Cook pasta in insta pot:**
    - Turn Instant Pot to “Rice” mode.
    - 2 cups (enough to just cover/submerge the pasta)
    - Add 1 tablespoon olive oil
    - 1/2 - 1 tsp salt
2. ** Cook vegetable base in Wok**
  - Add oil, add ural dal and heat it
  - add mustard seeds, when it starts popping
  - chopped onion, green chili, ginger garlic paste. Sauté until enough.
  - add butter
  - Add chopped tomato, pasta sauce, cook such that they meld well with onions 
  - Add carrot, capsicum, beans, boiled corn, and make sure vegetable are reasonably cooked and not raw
  - Add all spices (red chili powder, coriander powder, garam masala, salt), mix well.

2. **Add Pasta:**
    - Drain the cooked pasta
    
3. **Pressure Cook:**
    - Add the cooked pasta to Wok

4. **Finish & Garnish:**
    - stir pasta and vegetable base. I, sauté briefly till desired consistency.
    - Add grated cheese, powdered cashew, mixed herbs, chili flakes, and chopped coriander.
    - Stir and let cheese melt.

5. **Serve:** Enjoy hot straight from the pot.

**Tips:**

- Don’t overcook: Pasta can get mushy if cooked too long.
- For creamier texture, add cheese and a little butter after pressure cooking.
- If making a large batch, ensure enough liquid for pressure and pasta to cook evenly.
