# Indian Pantry Thai Curry Paste (Red & Yellow)

These Thai-style curry pastes are adapted for an Indian kitchen and work perfectly with milk + cashew–based tofu curry.

---

## 🔴 Thai Red Curry Paste (Indian Kitchen Version)

### Ingredients

- 4 cloves regular garlic
- 1 inch ginger
- 1 small onion OR 2 tbsp chopped onion
- 2 tsp red chili powder (Kashmiri chili ideal for color)
- 1 tsp coriander powder
- ½ tsp cumin powder
- ¼ tsp black pepper
- ½ tsp salt
- 1 tsp sugar
- 1 tbsp lemon or lime juice
- 1 tbsp oil
- 1 tbsp tomato paste (optional but recommended)

### Method

1. Add all ingredients to a blender.
2. Blend into a thick paste.
3. Add 1–2 tbsp water only if needed.
4. Sauté the paste in butter or oil for 1–2 minutes before adding milk or cashew cream.

**Flavor Profile:**  
Spicy, bold, and vibrant — best for tofu and vegetables.

---

## 🟡 Thai Yellow Curry Paste (Indian Kitchen Version)

### Ingredients

- 4 cloves garlic
- 1 inch ginger
- 2 tbsp chopped onion
- 1 tsp turmeric powder
- 1 tsp coriander powder
- ½ tsp cumin powder
- ¼ tsp chili powder (optional)
- ½ tsp black pepper
- ½ tsp salt
- 1 tsp sugar
- 1 tbsp lemon or lime juice
- 1 tbsp oil
- (Optional) 1 tbsp soaked cashews while blending

### Method

1. Blend all ingredients into a smooth paste.
2. Add a little water if necessary.
3. Sauté in oil or butter for 1–2 minutes before adding liquids.

**Flavor Profile:**  
Mild, slightly sweet, and creamy — very good for family-friendly curry.

---

## Which One Should You Choose?

| Preference                     | Choose    |
|--------------------------------|-----------|
| Restaurant-style spicy curry   | 🔴 Red    |
| Creamy comfort curry           | 🟡 Yellow |
| Kid-friendly mild curry        | 🟡 Yellow |
| Strong chili and garlic flavor | 🔴 Red    |

---

## Flavor Balancing Tip

For authentic Thai-style balance, adjust at the end:

- Add lime juice for brightness
- Add a pinch of sugar for balance
- Adjust salt as needed

Thai flavors balance: **spicy + salty + sweet + sour**

## Storage

- Refrigerator: Up to 1 week
- Freezer (ice cube tray): Up to 2 months
