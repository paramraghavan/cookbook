# 🥕 Vegetable Baking Guide (Indian Style)

## Base Baking Method

- **Temperature:** Preheat oven to **400°F (200°C)**
- **Mix:** Toss vegetables (or tofu) with **oil, turmeric, and salt**
- **Bake:** Flip halfway through cooking

---

## 🍠 Vegetable Baking Table

| Vegetable              | Temp  | Cut Size & Mix                                             | Bake Time |
|------------------------|-------|------------------------------------------------------------|-----------|
| **Sweet Potato**       | 400°F | 1-inch cubes, toss with oil, turmeric, salt                | 20–25 min |
| **Potato**             | 400°F | 1-inch cubes, toss with oil, turmeric, salt                | 25–30 min |
| **Carrot**             | 400°F | Thick rounds or sticks, toss with oil, turmeric, salt      | 20–25 min |
| **Cauliflower**        | 400°F | Medium florets, toss with oil, turmeric, salt              | 20–25 min |
| **Broccoli**           | 400°F | Large florets, toss with oil, turmeric, salt               | 18–22 min |
| **Green Beans**        | 400°F | Whole or halved, toss with oil, turmeric, salt             | 15–20 min |
| **Brussels Sprouts**   | 400°F | Halved, toss with oil, turmeric, salt                      | 20–25 min |
| **Zucchini**           | 400°F | Thick half-moons, toss with oil, turmeric, salt            | 15–18 min |
| **Bell Peppers**       | 400°F | Thick strips, toss with oil, turmeric, salt                | 15–18 min |
| **Eggplant (Baingan)** | 400°F | 1-inch cubes, toss with oil, turmeric, salt                | 20–25 min |
| **Okra (Bhindi)**      | 400°F | Whole (dry well), toss with oil, turmeric, salt            | 20–25 min |
| **Pressed Firm Tofu**  | 400°F | 1-inch cubes (well-pressed), toss with oil, turmeric, salt | 25–30 min |

---

## 🌟 General Baking Rules

- Press tofu for **15–30 minutes** before baking to remove moisture
- Do **not overcrowd the tray**
- Use parchment paper for easy cleanup
- Flip halfway
- For extra crispiness → bake last **5 minutes at 425°F**

# After Baking: Indian Flavor Options

---

## 🧡 North Indian Version (Masala Style)

### Tadka / Tempering

Heat **1–2 tbsp oil**, then add:

- ½ tsp cumin seeds
- Pinch hing (asafoetida)
- ½ tsp coriander powder
- ½ tsp red chili powder
- ½ tsp garam masala
- Optional: 1 tsp amchur or squeeze lemon

### Finish

Add baked vegetables/tofu and toss for **2–3 minutes**.

**Optional additions**

- Kasuri methi
- Chaat masala
- Finely chopped sautéed onions

**Best served with:** Roti, dal, jeera rice

---

## 🌿 South Indian Version (Poriyal / Thoran Style)

### Tadka / Tempering

Heat **coconut oil**, then add:

- ½ tsp mustard seeds
- ½ tsp urad dal
- ½ tsp chana dal
- 1 dried red chili
- Curry leaves
- Pinch hing

### Mix in:
- Fresh grated coconut
- Optional squeeze of lemon
