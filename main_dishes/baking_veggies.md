📝 Ingredients
2 medium sweet potatoes (peeled & cubed small)

2 tbsp coconut oil (best flavor)

½ tsp mustard seeds

½ tsp urad dal

½ tsp chana dal (optional but nice crunch)

1–2 dried red chilies (broken)

8–10 curry leaves

¼ tsp turmeric

½ tsp red chili powder (optional)

Salt to taste

3–4 tbsp freshly grated coconut

1 tsp lemon juice (optional)

👩🏽‍🍳 Method
Prep
Cut sweet potatoes into small cubes so they cook faster.

Tempering (Tadka)
Heat coconut oil in a kadai.
Add mustard seeds → let splutter.
Add urad dal & chana dal → fry till golden.
Add dried red chilies & curry leaves.

Add Sweet Potatoes
Add cubes, turmeric, and salt. Mix well.

Cook
Sprinkle 2–3 tbsp water.
Cover and cook on low for 10–12 minutes.
Stir occasionally until soft but not mushy.

Finish
Add grated coconut. Mix gently.
Cook uncovered for 2 minutes.
Add lemon juice if you like a slight tang.

🌿 Optional Add-Ons
Add a pinch of hing (asafoetida) for extra aroma

Add crushed pepper instead of chili powder for a Kerala touch

For a Tamil-style twist, add ½ tsp sambar powder near the end
