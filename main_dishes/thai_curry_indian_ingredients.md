# Creamy Cashew Thai-Style Tofu Curry with Rice

## Ingredients

### Rice (Choose One)

**Option 1 – Basmati Rice**

- 1 cup basmati rice
- 2 cups water
- Pinch salt

**Option 2 – Sona Masoori Rice**

- 1 cup sona masoori rice
- 2¼ cups water
- Pinch salt

---

### Baked Tofu

- 14 oz firm tofu (pressed)
- 1 tbsp oil
- ½ tsp chili powder
- ½ tsp salt
> see baking veggies
---

### Cashew Cream (Coconut Milk Substitute)

- ¼ cup raw cashews
- ¾ cup warm whole milk

---

### Curry Base

- 1 tbsp butter
- 1 tsp oil
- 3–4 cloves fresh garlic (finely minced)
- 1 tsp grated ginger (optional)
- 2 tbsp Thai curry paste (red or yellow)
- 1 cup mixed vegetables (bell pepper, carrot, broccoli, beans)
- ½ tsp sugar
- Salt to taste
- Squeeze of fresh lime

> for thai curry paste see thai_red_yellow_curry_paste_indian_style
---

## Instructions

### 1. Cook the Rice

1. Rinse rice 2–3 times.
2. Combine rice, water, and salt.
3. Simmer covered:
    - Basmati: 12–15 minutes
    - Sona Masoori: 15–18 minutes
4. Rest 5 minutes and fluff.

---

### 2. Bake the Tofu

1. Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C).
2. Cut pressed tofu into cubes.
3. Toss with oil, chili powder, and salt.
4. Bake 25–30 minutes, flipping halfway.

---

### 3. Prepare Cashew Cream

1. Soak cashews in warm water 10–15 minutes.
2. Drain.
3. Blend with ¾ cup warm milk until smooth and creamy.

---

### 4. Make the Curry

1. Heat butter and oil in a pan.
2. Add minced garlic (and ginger if using).
3. Sauté 30–40 seconds until fragrant.
4. Add Thai curry paste and cook 1 minute.
5. Lower heat.
6. Slowly pour in cashew-milk blend while stirring.
7. Add vegetables.
8. Simmer gently 6–8 minutes (do not boil hard).
9. Add sugar, salt, and lime juice.
10. Adjust seasoning to taste.

---

### 5. Combine

Add baked tofu to curry.
Simmer 2–3 minutes to absorb flavor.

---

## Serving

Serve curry over hot rice.
Garnish with fresh herbs or crushed peanuts if desired.

---

## Notes

- If curry is too thick → add 2 tbsp warm milk.
- If too thin → simmer a few extra minutes.
- For extra richness → add 1 tsp peanut butter.
