# Indian Style Pasta

**Ingredients:**

- **Pasta:** 2 cups (spiral, fusilli, or any)
- **Water:** Enough to boil pasta (about 4-5 cups), and for the Instant Pot, 2 cups (enough to just cover pasta and veggies)
- **Salt:** 1 tsp for boiling pasta + 1/2 tsp for masala (adjust to taste)
- **Oil:** 1 tbsp (any neutral oil)
- **Butter:** 1 tbsp

**Vegetables & Base:**
- **Onion:** 1 medium, chopped
- **Green Chilis:** 1-2, finely chopped (optional, adjust to preferred spice)
- **Ginger Garlic Paste:** 2 tsp (or 2 tsp finely chopped; garlic optional)
- **Carrot:** 1 small, chopped
- **Capsicum (Bell Pepper):** 1 small, chopped
- **Boiled Corn:** 2-3 tbsp
- **Beans:** 4-5, chopped (optional)
- **Tomato:** 1 medium, chopped
- **Pasta Sauce:** 1/2 cup to 1 cup
- **Cashew**: 5-10, powder it (optional)

**Spices:**
- **Red Chili Powder:** 1 tsp (adjust for heat)
- **Coriander Powder:** 1/2 tsp
- **Garam Masala:** 1/4 tsp

**Sauce & Finish:**
- **Pasta Sauce (or Tomato Sauce):** 2-3 tbsp
- **Grated Cheese:** 1/4 to 1/2 cup(or more for topping)
- **Fresh Coriander:** 2 tbsp, chopped
- **Mixed Herbs Seasoning:** 1/2 tsp
- **Red Chili Flakes:** 1/2 tsp (optional, for garnish)

Adjust veggie amounts and spice to taste and as per what you have! This will make a balanced, flavorful masala pasta.

**Steps:**

1. **Sauté Vegetables & Masala Base:**
    - Turn Instant Pot to “Sauté” mode.
    - Add oil and butter; when hot, add chopped onion, green chili, ginger garlic paste. Sauté until enough.
    - Add carrot, capsicum, beans, boiled corn, and continue to sauté for 2-3 minutes.
    - Add chopped tomato and pasta sauce. Cook for a minute.
    - Add all spices (red chili powder, coriander powder, garam masala, salt), mix well.

2. **Add Pasta:**
    - Add **uncooked pasta** directly to the pot.
    - Pour enough water (**about 2 cups or enough to just cover the pasta**, less if using juicy tomato puree).
    - Add tomato sauce and mix everything.

3. **Pressure Cook:**
    - Close lid, set valve to “Sealing.”
    - Cook on **Manual/Pressure Cook** for **4 minutes** (for regular pasta; if using whole wheat or thicker pasta, up
      to 5-6 min).
    - Quick release pressure once timer is done.

4. **Finish & Garnish:**
    - Open lid, stir pasta. If liquid remains, sauté briefly till desired consistency.
    - Add grated cheese, cashew powder, mixed herbs, chili flakes, and chopped coriander.
    - Stir and let cheese melt.

5. **Serve:** Enjoy hot straight from the pot.

**Tips:**

- Don’t overcook: Pasta can get mushy if cooked too long.
- For creamier texture, add cheese and a little butter after pressure cooking.
- If making a large batch, ensure enough liquid for pressure and pasta to cook evenly.
