# Paneer Tikka Masala

A rich and creamy North Indian curry with grilled cottage cheese in a tomato-based gravy.

## Ingredients

### For Paneer Tikka
- 400g paneer, cubed
- 1 cup yogurt
- 1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste
- 1 teaspoon red chili powder
- 1/2 teaspoon turmeric
- 1 teaspoon garam masala
- Salt to taste
- 1 bell pepper, cubed
- 1 onion, cubed

### For Masala Gravy
- 3 tablespoons oil or butter
- 1 large onion, finely chopped
- 2 teaspoons ginger-garlic paste
- 4 large tomatoes, pureed
- 1 teaspoon red chili powder
- 1 teaspoon coriander powder
- 1/2 teaspoon turmeric
- 1 teaspoon garam masala
- 1/2 cup heavy cream
- 2 tablespoons kasuri methi (dried fenugreek leaves)
- Salt to taste
- Fresh coriander for garnish

## Instructions

### Marinate and Grill Paneer
1. Mix yogurt with all spices, ginger-garlic paste, and salt
2. Add paneer cubes, bell pepper, and onion
3. Marinate for at least 30 minutes
4. Thread onto skewers or place on a baking tray
5. Grill or bake at 200°C (400°F) for 15-20 minutes until lightly charred
6. Set aside

### Make the Gravy
1. Heat oil in a large pan
2. Add chopped onions, sauté until golden brown
3. Add ginger-garlic paste, cook for 2 minutes
4. Add tomato puree, cook until oil separates (about 10 minutes)
5. Add all spices (chili, coriander, turmeric, garam masala)
6. Cook for 5 minutes, stirring frequently
7. Add cream and kasuri methi
8. Simmer for 5 minutes
9. Add the grilled paneer tikka
10. Cook for another 5 minutes
11. Garnish with fresh coriander

## Cooking Time
- Prep: 20 minutes
- Marinate: 30 minutes
- Cook: 40 minutes
- Total: 90 minutes

## Servings
4 servings

## Serving Suggestions
- Serve hot with naan bread or basmati rice
- Pair with cucumber raita
- Great for dinner parties!

## Tips
- For a smoky flavor, add a piece of charcoal smoking at the end
- Adjust cream amount based on desired richness
- Can substitute paneer with tofu for a vegan version (use coconut yogurt)
- Make the gravy ahead and add grilled paneer just before serving

## Nutrition (Per Serving)
- Calories: 420
- Protein: 18g
- Carbs: 25g
- Fat: 28g

## Storage
Refrigerate for up to 3 days. Reheat gently, adding a splash of water if needed.
