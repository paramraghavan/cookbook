# Takoda

_Takoda is pakoda made with Tofu_

## Easy Tofu Pakoda Recipe

### Ingredients

* 1 block (16 oz) tofu
* 1 cup besan (gram flour)
* 2 tbsp rice flour (or cornstarch for extra crispiness)
* 1 tsp red chili powder
* ½ tsp turmeric
* 1 tsp cumin powder - optional
* 1 tsp coriander powder - optional
* ½ tsp garam masala - optional
* 1 tsp ajwain (carom seeds) – optional but recommended
* 1 tsp salt
* 1 tsp grated ginger
* 1–2 chopped green chilies
* 2 tbsp chopped cilantro
* ½ Onion chopped small
* About ¾ cup water
* Oil for frying

## Step 1: Press the tofu

1. Remove the tofu from the package.
2. Wrap it in paper towels or a clean kitchen towel.
3. Place a heavy pan or cutting board on top.
4. Let it sit for **15–20 minutes**.

## Step 2: Cut the tofu

Cut into:

* 1-inch or little smaller cubes, or
* Finger-sized strips.
*

## Step 3: Make the batter

Mix together:

* 1 cup besan
* 2 tbsp rice flour
* Chili powder
* Turmeric
* Cumin powder
* Coriander powder
* Garam masala
* Ajwain
* Salt
* Ginger
* Green chilies
* Cilantro

Slowly add water while whisking until you get a **thick pancake-like batter**.
> Let the batter sit for 10 minutes, if it's too dry add some more water to get the thick pancake consistency

## Step 4: Coat the tofu

Add the tofu pieces and gently mix until every piece is coated.

## Step 5: Fry

1. Heat oil over medium heat.
2. Drop the coated tofu pieces carefully into the oil.
3. Fry **3–4 minutes per side** until deep golden brown.
4. Remove and drain on paper towels.

## Step 6: Serve

Serve hot with:

* Mint-coriander chutney
* Tamarind chutney
* Tomato ketchup
* Masala chai

### For extra-crispy pakodas

* Add **1 tablespoon hot oil** to the batter before mixing.
* Let the batter rest for **10 minutes** before coating the tofu.
* Fry on **medium heat** (not high), so the inside heats through while the outside becomes crisp.

### Tofu

The tofu I sued (Nasoya Refrigerated Organic Super Firm Tofu, 16 oz)
is [Marketside Organic Firm Tofu](https://www.walmart.com/ip/Nasoya-Refrigerated-Organic-Super-Firm-Tofu-16-oz/134768563),
16 oz, which works well for pakodas. If it's firm (not extra firm),
I recommend pressing it for 15–20 minutes first to remove excess moisture for crispier pakodas.
