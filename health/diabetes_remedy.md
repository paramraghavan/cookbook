2.  **Fiber First:** Eat your veggies (like Broccoli Sprouts or a salad). This creates a "mesh" in your small intestine.

## 4. ⚠️ Critical Safety: The "Synergy Warning"
Because these items—particularly **Vinegar, Cinnamon, and Fenugreek**—are so effective at lowering blood sugar, they can be dangerous if you are already on medication.

* **The Risk:** "Stacking" these natural remedies on top of **Insulin or Metformin** can lead to **Hypoglycemia** (dangerously low blood sugar).
* **The Rule:** Never start this full protocol without consulting your doctor, especially if you are already taking glucose-lowering drugs.
