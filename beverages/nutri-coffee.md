If you're making a **Nutri (or Nutri-style) coffee protein shake** for a **long car ride**, you can make it both tastier and keep it cold longer.

### Basic recipe
- 1 bottle or serving of Nutri coffee shake
- ½ cup cold milk (or almond/oat milk)
- 4–5 ice cubes
- Blend well.

### To make it taste even better
- ☕ Add **½ teaspoon instant coffee** for a stronger coffee flavor.
- 🍦 Add **½ teaspoon vanilla extract** for a café-style taste.
- 🍫 Add **1 teaspoon unsweetened cocoa powder** or chocolate syrup for a mocha flavor.
- 🍌 Blend in **½ frozen banana** for a creamy texture.
- 🧂 Add a **tiny pinch of cinnamon** or nutmeg for warmth.
- 🥜 Add **1 tablespoon peanut butter** or almond butter for richness.

### For a long car ride
- Use a **stainless-steel insulated tumbler** and pre-chill it with ice water for 5–10 minutes.
- If possible, freeze the shake for **30–45 minutes** before leaving so it's slushy when you start.
- Keep it in a cooler bag with an ice pack if you'll be driving for several hours.
