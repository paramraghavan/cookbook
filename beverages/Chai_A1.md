# A1-Style Chai with Tata Chakra Gold ☕

Making **A1-style chai** with **Tata Chakra Gold Tea** is easy — and Chakra Gold is great for strong, *kadak* chai
because it has a bold Assam blend.

---

## Ingredients (1 cup – strong & kadak)

- 1 cup water
- ¾ cup milk (adjust based on how strong you like it)
- 1½ teaspoons Tata Chakra Gold tea ( or any other tea)
- 1½–2 teaspoons sugar (adjust to taste)
- ½ inch fresh ginger (crushed) – optional but recommended
- pinch of tulsi - optional
- 1 crushed cardamom – optional

---

## Step-by-Step Method

### 1️⃣ Boil the Water

Add water to a saucepan.  
Add crushed ginger, tulsi and cardamom if using.  
Bring to a rolling boil.

---

### 2️⃣ Add Tea Powder

Add **1½ teaspoons Tata Chakra Gold**.  
Let it boil for **1–2 minutes** until the water becomes dark reddish-brown.

> **Tip:** For A1 roadside style, let it boil a little longer for extra strength.

---

### 3️⃣ Add Milk

Pour in milk.  
Let it rise and boil for **2–3 minutes** on medium heat.

⚠️ Let it rise once or twice (don’t let it spill). This gives that rich flavor.

---

### 4️⃣ Add Sugar

Add sugar and stir well.  
Simmer another 30 seconds.

---

### 5️⃣ Strain & Serve

Strain into a cup.  
Serve hot.

---

## Pro Tips for True “A1 Hotel” Taste

- ✔️ Slightly overboil (but don’t burn)
- ✔️ Use full-fat milk
- ✔️ Don’t reduce tea quantity — Chakra Gold handles strong boiling well
- ✔️ For extra roadside flavor: add a tiny pinch of crushed black pepper
